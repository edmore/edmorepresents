Let's talk stats ...

  • UCT has ~25,000 students
  • Production launch was in Feb 2012
  • 47 equipped venues and growing ...
  • ~240 recordings per week in 2015
  • Up to 2,500 students use recordings weekly

How do students and teachers engage with the media?

Feedback form

Feedback

In general students love the service, but there are requests :
  • Ability to see what's written on all blackboards
  • Recordings available faster
  • Better audio quality
  • Better camera picture quality
  • Side-by-side download option with camera and screen in a single file

How are we reacting to the Feedback? : Further Enhancement ...

  • In general the more we improve the system the better the user engagement and the better the learning experience ...
  • Camera steering options that can solve the blackboard problem e.g. lecturesight
  • Streamling the backend processes as well as the consent and trim processes in order to ensure recordings are published faster
  • Class renewal project to streamline AV equipment in venues

Continued ...

  • Recording at higher resolutions and purchasing of better cameras e.g 4K cameras
  • Streaming of HD media
  • Improving the mobile experience e.g. incorporating HTML5 players, such as Theodul and Paella
  • Providing easy to download and playback file formats e.g. side-by-side downloads

So what do we know ...

  • Still more work to be done ...
  • Students love the service
    • I wish all of my lectures were recorded. It adds so much value to my overall learning experience. I am able to work at my own pace in a work environment that is best suited to me at a time that suits me best ... The only real improvement that needs to be done is that more (all) venues and lectures should be recorded.

    ~ Student
